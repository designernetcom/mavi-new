About Mavi Energy

Leading the Way in Industrial Safety and Energy Savings.

At Mavi Energy, we specialize in delivering innovative energy-saving and industrial solutions that empower businesses to optimize efficiency, enhance safety, and reduce operational costs. Backed by over three decades of expertise, we are proud to be an industry leader, carrying forward the trusted legacy of S.S. Enterprises and our well-established brands, Autoweld/Weldon.

Our journey began with a vision to redefine industrial energy solutions, and today, we stand as a trusted partner for businesses looking to embrace sustainable practices and maximize productivity. From pioneering energy-saving devices to offering advanced welding solutions, we are committed to providing cutting-edge products tailored to the ever-evolving needs of industries.

With models supporting input currents of 32 Amps, 63 Amps, and 95 Amps, we provide customized recommendations for various types of welding machines and applications. Backed by a commitment to quality and innovation, Mavi Energy is your trusted partner for sustainable and high-performing industrial solutions.
