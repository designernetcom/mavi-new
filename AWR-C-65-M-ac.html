    AWR-C-65-M (Three phase with Ammeter and Volt Meter) (AC Type)


    The AWR-C-65-M is a three-phase solid-state relay (SSR) or AC power controller equipped with an integrated ammeter and voltmeter for real-time monitoring. It is designed to control and monitor high-power AC loads with a maximum capacity of 63 amps per phase, making it ideal for demanding industrial applications.

  • Electrical Specifications:

    Typically operates within 4–32V DC or 90–280V AC depending on control voltage options.

    Supports 240V AC, 415V AC, or 480V AC, meeting common three-phase system requirements.

    Rated for up to 63A continuous current per phase.

    Designed for 47–63 Hz AC power systems.

    Rated at 2500V RMS or higher for superior electrical insulation and protection.

  • Protection Features:

    Shields the relay from sudden voltage spikes and fluctuations.

    External circuit breakers or fuses are recommended for additional safety.

    Built to endure short-term high inrush currents.

    Requires an appropriately rated heatsink or forced cooling system for efficient heat management.
