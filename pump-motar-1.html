Mavi Make Timers for 2 Motor Application with DOL Starter , Over Load Relay , Indicators with separate On Time/OFF time programmable for Each Motors Model :- MAVI-AUTO-2 M

Details:


  • The MAVI-AUTO-2M Timer is designed for controlling two motors or pumps in applications requiring programmable ON/OFF timing with protection features.

  • Key Features:

    Dual-channel timer control (Motor 1 & Motor 2)

    Independent ON/OFF time settings for each motor

    Integrated DOL starter control for motor starting

    Overload relay compatibility for motor protection

    LED indicators for system status

    Suitable for industrial pumps, motors, and automation systems

  • Protection Features:

    Integrated Overload Relay trips the motor in case of excess current draw.

    Indicator lights provide quick visual alerts for overload or system faults.

    Independent ON/OFF timing ensures controlled motor sequencing.

  • Typical Applications:

    Water Pumps for irrigation systems

    Industrial motors in conveyor systems

    HVAC control systems

    Automation setups requiring precise timing control
